Coin of the Day!

The 1944 zinc coated steel cent is a "Transitional Error" which occurred during the changeover from zinc coated steel back to bronze. This makes the coin super unique and not many exist; however you should always check your 1943 coins to see if they're magnetic just in case you have one of the few coins that exist!
